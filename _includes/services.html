Structure

Three Phase Study Process

Learn

During the week you will gather information and share here what you have found. You will do use cases of code from what you have learned

Collaborate

Each Saturday we will come together and share what we have learned during the week and if there is code, we will do code reviews to learn from what others have done.

Projects

At the beginning of the month it will be determined what type of project each person will do. At the end of the month those projects will be presented.
